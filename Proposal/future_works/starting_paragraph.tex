%My future work will build on the two strategies I have developed---embedding domain knowledge and adaptively collecting data---while extending them beyond single, well-defined modalities. As scientific measurements become increasingly \textit{\textbf{multimodal}}, new challenges arise in assimilating and designing across sources. I will also critique these strategies by asking: to what extent can scaling---training larger models on more data---substitute for, or complement, careful domain knowledge integration and experimental design?

Building on my existing work above developing methods that embed domain structure in inferring stochastic differential equations (SDEs), I now aim to extend these principles to settings with perturbations and relax the requirement of computationally heavy SDE simulations.
